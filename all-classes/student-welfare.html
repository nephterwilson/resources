🏥 Malawi Health Resources & Contacts

Emergency Health Contacts:

  • Emergency Services: 199 or 998
  • AIDS Helpline: 80077 (Toll-free)
  • Mental Health Support: Contact nearest health center
  • Gender-based Violence: 902 (Police hotline)
🏥 Major Health Facilities:
  • Kamuzu Central Hospital (Lilongwe)
  • Queen Elizabeth Central Hospital (Blantyre)
  • Mzuzu Central Hospital
  • Zomba Central Hospital
  • Local district hospitals and health centers
🤝 Support Organizations:
  • NAC (National AIDS Commission)
  • Banja La Mtsogolo (Family planning)
  • Partners in Hope
  • Malawi Red Cross Society
  • Local NGOs and community health workers
