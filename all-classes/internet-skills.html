Stay Safe Online

  • Never share passwords or personal information
  • Use strong passwords with letters, numbers, symbols
  • Be careful of scams and fake news
  • Don't click on suspicious links or downloads
  • Tell a trusted adult if something feels wrong

Be a Good Digital Citizen

  • Be respectful and kind in all online interactions
  • Don't spread false information or rumors
  • Respect other people's privacy and property
  • Help create a safe internet community
  • Report inappropriate behavior to authorities

  • Use quotation marks for exact phrases

Finding Trusted Information
