Why Assessment Tools Matter

Assessment tools help teachers monitor student progress, identify learning gaps, and tailor instruction. In Malawi’s secondary schools, they are essential for preparing students for JCE, MSCE, and other national examinations, ensuring equitable learning opportunities.

Types of Assessment Tools

Formative Assessments

Quizzes, assignments, and in-class exercises used to monitor student learning regularly.

Summative Assessments

End-of-term tests, mock exams, and standardized testing for final evaluations.

Digital & Interactive Tools

Online quizzes, LMS platforms, and mobile-based revision apps for self-paced learning.
